The missing data for the fire data can be found at: https://cwfis.cfs.nrcan.gc.ca/datamart/download/nbac

The year with missing data is 2019, you can download that year and replace the files in data/2019 folder. 

The data for the provinces shapes for making the map of canada is also missing as it was too large to upload to
git hub. 

This can be found and downloaded at: https://library.carleton.ca/find/gis/geospatial-data/shapefiles-canada-united-states-and-world

once downloaded they can be placed in the data/provinces folder.
